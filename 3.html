ABOUT THE TIMER

A "Pomodoro timer" refers to a time management technique where you work on a task for focused 25-minute intervals ("pomodoros") separated by short breaks, typically 5 minutes, and after every four pomodoros, you take a longer break (around 20-30 minutes)
